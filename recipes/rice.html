A dish that can be served alongside anything, great for complementing the flavor of a meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of washed rice
  • 2 cups of boiling water
  • 1 clove of crushed garlic
  • 1/4 of a chopped onion
  • enough olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Saut√© the garlic and onion in olive oil.
  2. Add the rice and let it fry for about 30 seconds.
  3. Add the boiling water and salt.
  4. Lower the heat and let it cook until the water has almost evaporated.
  5. Cover the pot and wait about 20 minutes before serving.
