Lasagna bolognese is a typical and great food, it's a common lasagna with meat, it's very easy to preapare. it's originaly from Bologna, Italy, but actually, that recipe can be found anywhere in the world.

Ingredients

  • 500g of lasagna noodles
  • 500g of ground beef
  • 2 boxes of heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 3 tablespoons of wheat flour
  • 500g of ham
  • 500 g of mozzarella
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 glasses of milk
  • 1 grated onion
  • 3 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 3 cloves of crushed garlic
  • 1 packet of grated cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the manufacturer's instructions, pour it into a baking dish with ice water to prevent sticking, and set aside.
  2. Sauté the garlic, onion, ground beef, and tomato sauce; let it cook for 3 minutes and set aside.
  3. Melt the margarine, add the 3 tablespoons of wheat flour and stir.
  4. Pour the milk in gradually and continue stirring.
  5. Add the cream, stir for 1 minute, and turn off the heat.
  6. Pour some of the Bolognese sauce into a baking dish, then half of the pasta, half of the ham, half of the mozzarella, all of the white sauce, and the remaining pasta.
  7. Repeat the layers until you reach the rim of the container.
  8. Finish with the grated cheese and bake in a preheated oven (220°C) for about 20 minutes.
