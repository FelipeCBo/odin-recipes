A delicious chocolate cake, a simple and great recipe, perfect for when you have a craving for something sweet.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of sugar
  • 2 cups of wheat flour
  • 1 cup of cocoa powder or chocolate drink mix
  • 1/2 cup of oil
  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 cup of hot water
  • 4 tablespoons of milk
  • 1/2 cup (tea) of cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1 cup of sugar

Steps

  1. In a blender, beat together the eggs, sugar, oil, chocolate powder, and wheat flour.
  2. Pour the dough into a bowl and add the warm water and yeast, mixing well.
  3. Pour the batter into a greased pan and bake in a preheated medium-high oven (200Â°C) for 40 minutes.
  4. Remove from the mold while still warm.
  5. In a saucepan, bring all the ingredients to a boil.
  6. Pour it over the cake while it's still hot.
